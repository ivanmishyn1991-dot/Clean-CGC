# Work Photos - Before and After

## How to add your own photos

1. Place your photos in this folder: `/public/assets/images/work-photos/`
2. Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP
3. Photos will automatically appear in the "Before and after" sidebar on service and city pages
4. Maximum 4 photos will be displayed (first 4 files alphabetically)
5. Name your files clearly, e.g.: `01-before-gutter.jpg`, `02-after-gutter.jpg`

## If folder is empty
The website will show stock placeholder images from Unsplash.

## Recommended photo sizes
- Width: 600-1200px
- Aspect ratio: Square or slightly vertical works best
- File size: Under 500KB for fast loading
